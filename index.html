About Kanchan

The Artist Behind The Brushes

Kanchan is a leading makeover artist based in Didwana, Nagaur, with over 12 years of experience in bridal and fashion makeup. Her creative touch and commitment to excellence have earned her a trusted reputation across Rajasthan.

Blending her artistic vision with refined techniques, Kanchan curates looks that highlight natural beauty with grace. She is married to Mukesh Sevda and is a proud mother to her daughter, Tanu.

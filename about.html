About Kanchan

From Fine Arts grad to Rajasthan’s most sought-after bridal artist, Kanchan has spent 12+ years perfecting the art of transformation.

Based in Didwana, Nagaur, her serene studio combines tradition, precision and a deeply personal touch.

Wife to Mukesh Sevda and mom to Tanu, she balances family with a thriving career, mentoring the next generation of makeup pros.
